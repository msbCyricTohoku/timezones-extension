Privacy Policy for timezones-extension

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Thank you for using timezones-extension. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the extension accesses and how it's used.

Information We Access

To function correctly, timezones-extension needs to access:

  • Your system's current time: Used to calculate the time in different configured time zones.
  • Your system's local time zone setting: Used to accurately display your local time within the extension popup.

How Information is Used

The accessed information (system time and local time zone) is used solely for the purpose of calculating and displaying the current time in various locations directly within the extension's popup window when you open it.

Data Collection, Storage, and Sharing

timezones-extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or Browse activity.

  • We do not save your time, time zone, or any other information.
  • We do not send any data to external servers.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • All calculations happen locally on your device.
